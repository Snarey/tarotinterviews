July 6, 2026

Welcome to the heart of the Tarot Interviews project! Whether we are drawing cards over a Riverside call to the other side of the world or pulling them via a messaging app, our three-card, three-question format has conjured up incredible conversations with a diverse range of writers, artists, musicians, and cultural figures.

This page is a living index celebrating everyone who has shared their time, energy, and sense of wonder with us. Below, you will find the podcast guests and written interviewees who make it all happen.

At a Glance

  • Tarot Interviews (Podcast): 40 featured guests and contributors
  • Tarot DMs (Written): 32 written interview guests
  • Our Community Total: 72 creative voices across both projects

Familiar Faces We have had the absolute pleasure of featuring incredible talents, including Joanne Harris, Adrian Tchaikovsky, Lauren Beukes, Carrie Jenkins, Julian Simpson, Sacha Coward, Lucy Rose, Alice Slater, Amy Blackthorn, Kit Whitfield, Beans on Toast, Jess Silk, Amelia Coburn, and GANS.

FAQ

How many people have joined the project so far? We have welcomed approximately 72 amazing guests and contributors across both Tarot Interviews and Tarot DMs.

What is the difference between Tarot Interviews and Tarot DMs? It all comes down to the medium:

  • Tarot Interviews: Our audio podcast where we pull cards and chat on air.
  • Tarot DMs: Our written interview series, adapting a similar three-card structure but entirely through messaging apps for a cosier conversation.

Do you include festival contributors in the directory? Absolutely. Our festival specials are a huge part of what we do. We list all our named artists, artisan traders, makers, crew members, performers, and festival-goers as contributors to celebrate the community outside the standard studio setting.

Why is the total number of guests listed as "approximate"? Festivals are wonderfully chaotic! A single festival episode might feature one-on-one interviews alongside quick chats with traders, crew members, photographed contributors, and grouped festival voices. We use a close estimate for our total guest count.

Where can I dive into the series?

  • Listen: You can stream Tarot Interviews on all major podcast platforms, including Apple Podcasts and Spotify.

  • Read: You can explore the Tarot DMs written series over at tarotdms.com
