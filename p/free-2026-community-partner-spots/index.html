    Free 2026 Community Partner Spots

    By Finbarre Snarey June 25, 2026

    Tarot Interviews is opening a small number of free community partner spots for tarot, folklore, heritage, arts and culture organizations.

    Selected partners can share a finished 30 second audio message with Tarot Interviews listeners. There is no fee. In return, I ask partners to help spread the word with an Instagram follow, social post, tag or link where possible.

    UK-based projects, podcasts and cultural or heritage work may be prioritised, but international submissions are very welcome.

    Please send finished audio only, as a WAV or high quality MP3.

    Spots are limited and all submissions will be reviewed before approval.
