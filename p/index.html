Oh no. You drew The Tower card...

How would you use it to describe your current project?

Welcome to Tarot Interviews, a world-first podcast format that throws away the journalist's playbook! Artists, writers, musicians and all kinds of creative minds are interviewed using a tarot deck.

Every episode is completely unscripted and entirely unique. Each guest draws three cards, and those cards become our prompts.
